Not Sleeping
Can Be Deadly

It is estimated that more than half of all adults in Massachusetts are not getting the recommended amount of sleep. And, the results are serious. People suffering from sleep apnea are 3x’s more likely to be involved in a fatal car crash. Inadequate sleep makes you 23x’s more likely to suffer a heart attack, and even mild sleep apnea can increase your chances of suffering a stroke, getting diabetes or experiencing significant weight gain. It is not a joke. Sleep apnea can kill you, and there is no time to waste.
